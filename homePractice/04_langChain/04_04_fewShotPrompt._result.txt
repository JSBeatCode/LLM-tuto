▶ [5] LLM 호출 — Step-by-step 추론 유도
============================================================
[질문]
What is the age of the director of a movie which got a best international film in Oscar in 2010?

[LLM 응답 — Few-Shot으로 유도된 Step-by-step 추론]
Are follow up questions needed here: Yes.  
Follow up: What is the title of the movie that won the Best International Feature Film Oscar in 2010?  
Intermediate Answer: The title of the movie is "The Secret in Their Eyes."  
Follow up: Who is the director of "The Secret in Their Eyes"?  
Intermediate Answer: The director is Juan José Campanella.  
Follow up: What is the birthdate of Juan José Campanella?  
Intermediate Answer: Juan José Campanella was born on July 19, 1953.  
Follow up: How old was Juan José Campanella in 2010?  
Intermediate Answer: Juan José Campanella was 57 years old in 2010.  
So the final answer is: 57

Intermediate Answer: '쉰들러 리스트'의 주연 배우는 리암 니슨입니다.  
So the final answer is: 리암 니슨

▶ [8] Zero-Shot vs Few-Shot 비교
============================================================
[공통 질문]
Who lived longer, Cleopatra or Julius Caesar?

[Zero-Shot 응답 — 예시 없이 바로 질문]
Cleopatra lived longer than Julius Caesar. Cleopatra VII was born in 69 BC and died in 30 BC, making her 39 years old at the time of her death. Julius Caesar was born in 100 BC and was assassinated in 44 BC, which means he lived to be 55 years old. Therefore, Cleopatra lived for a shorter period than Julius Caesar.

[Few-Shot 응답 — 예시 2개 제공 후 같은 질문]
Are follow up questions needed here: Yes.  
Follow up: How old was Cleopatra when she died?  
Intermediate answer: Cleopatra was 39 years old when she died.  
Follow up: How old was Julius Caesar when he died?  
Intermediate answer: Julius Caesar was 55 years old when he died.  
So the final answer is: Julius Caesar

💡 핵심 인사이트:
   - Zero-Shot : LLM이 자유롭게 답변 → 형식이 제각각
   - Few-Shot  : 예시를 보고 패턴을 학습 → Follow up / Intermediate answer
                 형식을 그대로 따라 Step-by-step으로 추론
   → 복잡한 추론이 필요한 질문일수록 Few-Shot의 효과가 커집니다.

# 🧠 감으로 이해 (진짜 중요)

## 🍔 비유

### 1️⃣ ChatPromptTemplate

> “이 대화는 이렇게 진행돼”

```text
system: 너는 친절한 상담사야
user: 질문
```

👉 **대화 구조를 만드는 틀**

---

### 2️⃣ Few-shot Prompt

> “이렇게 답하는 거야 (예시 보여줌)”

```text
예시1: 이렇게 답함
예시2: 이렇게 답함

이제 너 차례
```

👉 **답변 스타일을 학습시키는 방법**

---

# ⚔️ 핵심 차이 (진짜 중요🔥)

| 구분    | ChatPromptTemplate | FewShotPromptTemplate |
| ----- | ------------------ | --------------------- |
| 역할    | 구조 정의              | 학습(패턴 전달)             |
| 목적    | system/user 구분     | 예시 기반 학습              |
| 핵심    | “누가 말하냐”           | “어떻게 답하냐”             |
| 사용 위치 | 프롬프트 전체 틀          | 프롬프트 내부               |
| 관계    | 기반 구조              | 그 위에 올라감              |

---

👉 같이 쓰는 관계 ✅

# 🔥 실제 구조 (실무 모습)

이렇게 같이 쓴다 👇

```python
ChatPromptTemplate([
  ("system", "너는 보험 심사 전문가야"),
  ("user", """
    예시1:
    진단: 감기 → 지급 가능

    예시2:
    진단: 미용 → 지급 불가

    ---
    진단: {input}
    → 판단?
  """)
])
```

👉 이 안에 있는 예시 부분 = Few-shot
